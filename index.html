Our Mission

In NTR District, rising rental costs have pushed many hard-working families to the brink of homelessness. We connect property owners with available affordable units to families in need, creating sustainable housing solutions that benefit our entire community.

Our platform is a partnership between local government, non-profits, and compassionate landlords working together to keep our neighbors housed.
